Phase 4: Analysis

Breaking the Joke

"Could I BE wearing any more clothes?"

Why is this funny? Joey is making fun of how Chandler speaks.

Chandler always emphasizes the verb "BE" when he asks a question.

Cultural Note:

Joey says he is going "commando". This means he is not wearing underwear! 🩲❌ He is wearing Chandler's pants, but nothing under them!

Grammar Focus I

Present Continuous

We use verb TO BE + ING to talk about actions happening right now.

Affirmative

I am wearing a shirt.

He is wearing a tie.

Negative

I am not wearing pants.

He is not wearing a sweater.

Grammar Focus I

Asking Questions

To make a question, swap the subject and the Verb To Be.

You are wearing pants.

⬇️

Are you wearing pants?

Visual Practice

Grammar Focus II

Can & Could

We use CAN for present ability, and COULD for past ability or possibility.

Can (Present)

"I can wear your clothes."

Could (Possibility / Exaggeration)

"Could I be wearing any more clothes?"

Joey uses "Could" to say it is physically impossible to wear more clothes.
